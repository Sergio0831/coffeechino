About Us

We opened our award-winning Coffee Chino Cafe in 2016. We are opened 7 days a week. At Coffee Chino you will find cosy atmosphere, and whether you're seeking a quiet corner to catch up on work, meeting a friend for a chat, or simply looking for a space to relax, our coffee shop provides the ideal setting. With warm lighting, comfortable seating, and a friendly ambiance, we strive to create a haven where you can unwind, connect, and enjoy the simple pleasures of life. Visit us and enjoy real coffee and tasty food.
